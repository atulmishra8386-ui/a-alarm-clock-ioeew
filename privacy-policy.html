1. **Personal Stuff**

When you use our site or services, we might ask for things like:

*   Your Name
*   Email
*   Your number
*   Where you live
*   Payment details (if you're buying something)
*   Anything else you tell us through forms or when you sign up

2. **Automatic Stuff**

When you visit, we might automatically grab some info, such as:

*   Your IP address
*   The kind of browser you’re using
*   Your computer's operating system
*   Which pages you look at and how long you stay
*   Where you were before you got here
*   Device IDs
*   Cookies and stuff like that

**How We Put Your Info to Work**

We put the info we collect to work to:

*   Keep our site and services up and running
*   Answer your questions and help you out
*   Process your purchases and send you confirmations
*   Send newsletters and promos (you can say no anytime)
*   Figure out how people are using our site to make it better
*   Find and fix any tech or security problems
*   Follow the law

**Cookies and Other Trackers**

Our site uses cookies to make things better for you, see what people are doing on our site, and show you stuff you might like. Cookies are tiny files that sit on your device. You can turn them off in your browser, but some parts of our site might not work right if you do.

**How We Keep Your Info Safe**

We have safety measures to protect your personal info from being seen, changed, or wrecked by people who shouldn't. But keep in mind, nothing online is 100% safe, so we can't promise it's totally secure.

**Sharing Your Info**

We don't sell or rent your personal info to anyone. We might share it with helpers who run our site, but they have to keep it secret. We might also have to share if the law tells us to, or to protect our rights.

**Your Say and Your Choices**

Depending on where you live, you have rights about your personal info, like:

*   Seeing what info we have on you
*   Asking us to fix or delete your info
*   Saying no to marketing stuff
*   Telling us to stop using your info in some ways
*   Asking for your data in a portable format.


**Kids' Privacy**

Our site isn't for kids under 13. We don't knowingly grab info from kids. If we find out we have info from a kid without a parent's okay, we'll delete it.

**Changes to These Rules**

We might change this policy sometimes. Check back here now and then to see what's new. If you keep using the site after we change it, that means you're okay with the new rules.
